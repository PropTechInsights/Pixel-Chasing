About the Show

Pixel Chasing is a podcast about the people pushing boundaries in our digital world. Each episode is an in-depth conversation with a guest who's shaping how we live, work, and create in an ever-evolving landscape.

From technologists to visionaries, every conversation goes deep — exploring ideas, experiences, and the stories behind the people driving change.
